Luxelinks Limited partnered with the International Health Organization in the UK, to run a funded Training on INFECTION PREVENTION AND CONTROL for the Hospitality Industry.

Hospitality Establishments were selected to nominate some 2 of their staff, to attend the Training for FREE and the Training was endorsed by the MINISTRY OF TOURISM, ARTS AND CULTURE, LAGOS STATE.

The purpose of this Training was to support the Hospitality Industry and to ensure that we remain productive and soar even higher, regardless of the present status of the world, as many industries, including hospitality has been faced with lots of setbacks and challenges due to the COVID-19 Pandemic.
